About Me

Hello! I am Mattia Moscato. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Heritage Conservation and Management and am nearing completion of my Master's in Digital and Public Humanities. My expertise involves analyzing how cultural influences and audience dynamics are expressed through texts and images, with a specific focus on the effects of historical events and controversies on these interactions. I am also dedicated to the effective conservation, accessibility, and public communication of cultural heritage, employing both digital and traditional methods. In my free time, I enjoy video editing and digital storytelling.
